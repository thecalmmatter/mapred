Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kottayam at Valavoor, Pala, Kerala is one among the IIITs that have
been established as "Institutions of National Importance" by Ministry of Education, Govt. of India under the ambit
of IIIT (PPP) Act 2017. IIIT Kottayam campus is situated at Valavoor, Pala, Kottayam - a picturous location, spread
over in 53 acres of land - allotted by Govt. of Kerala in the southern part of the state commonly referred to as God's
Own Country. As one of a few Indian Institutes of repute, IIIT Kottayam offers specialized courses covering all major
research domains of computer science and engineering: IoT, Data science, Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence,
Machine learning, Parallel Computing, Brain simulation, HPC, Programming languages, Bioinformatics, and so forth,
as part of the 4 year B.Tech program. An M.Tech course is organized exclusively for the working professionals on
Artificial Intelligence & Data Science, Cyber Security, CSE with Specialization in Big Data & Machine Learning . In
addition, it offers Ph.D program in CSE/ECE/Mathematics.
